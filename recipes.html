Vegan Eggs Benedict

Ingredients

For the Vegan Hollandaise Sauce:

  • 6 ripe yellow tomatoes
  • 1⁄4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, plus extra for tossing with the tomatoes
  • 1 tsp. Dijon mustard
  • Pinch white pepper
  • 2 Tbsp. white vinegar or wine vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp. vegan margarine (try Earth Balance brand)
  • 2 tsp. arrowroot mixed in 1/4 cup cold water

    • For the ‘Eggs’ and ‘Bacon’:

  • 1 block extra-firm tofu, cut into 6 1/4-inch-thick slabs
  • 3 Tbsp. unbleached white flour
  • 1 Tbsp. nutritional yeast
  • Pinch turmeric
  • Garlic powder, to taste
  • 1⁄2 tsp. salt
  • 1⁄2 tsp. black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp. canola oil
  • 6 slices faux Canadian bacon (try Yves brand)

    • To serve:
  • 6 English muffins
  • 1 Tbsp. vegan margarine
  • 3 Tbsp. minced fresh chives

    • Directions

    For the Vegan Hollandaise Sauce:

    1. Preheat the oven to 300°F.
    2. Toss the tomatoes in some olive oil.
    3. Place on a baking sheet and roast for 30 minutes.
    4. Flip over and roast for another 30 minutes.
    5. Remove from the oven and place in a blender, pulsing to chop.
    6. Slowly add 1/4 cup of oil and mustard, white pepper, and vinegar, blending until emulsified.
    7. Strain through a sieve.
    8. Place the sauce in a medium sauté pan over medium heat.
    9. Add the vegan margarine and the arrowroot mixture and whisk until thickened.
    10. Taste and adjust the seasonings.*

    For the ‘Eggs’ and ‘Bacon’:

    1. Using a ring mold, cut the slabs of tofu into circles the same diameter as the English muffins.
    2. In a bowl, mix the flour, nutritional yeast, turmeric, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
    3. Dredge the tofu in the flour mixture and shake off any excess.
    4. In a medium pan, heat the canola oil over high heat.
    5. Fry each side of the tofu rounds until golden.
    6. Remove and place on paper towels to drain.
    7. In the same pan, cook the faux Canadian bacon for 1 to 2 minutes on each side and set aside.

    To serve:

    1. Toast the English muffins and spread with the vegan margarine.
    2. Top each half with one tofu round and one slice of the faux Canadian bacon.
    3. Place on serving plates.
    4. Spoon the Vegan Hollandaise Sauce on top.
    5. Sprinkle with the chives and serve immediately.

    Vegan Buddha Bowl

    Ingredients
    • 1 cup quinoa
    • 2 cups water
    • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp paprika
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1/2 tsp black pepper
    • 1 avocado, sliced
    • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
    • 1 cucumber, sliced
    • 1/4 cup hummus
    • 2 tbsp tahini
    • 1 tbsp lemon juice
    • 1 tbsp water
    • 1 tsp maple syrup
    Directions
    1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water.
    2. In a medium pot, bring the quinoa and water to a boil.
    3. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the water is absorbed.
    4. Remove from heat and let sit, covered, for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork.
    5. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
    6. In a bowl, toss the chickpeas with olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
    7. Spread the chickpeas on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes, or until crispy.
    8. In a small bowl, whisk together the tahini, lemon juice, water, and maple syrup to make the dressing.
    9. Divide the quinoa, chickpeas, avocado, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and hummus among four bowls.
    10. Drizzle with the tahini dressing and serve.

    Vegan Lentil Shepherd's Pie

    Ingredients
    • 1 cup green or brown lentils
    • 2 cups vegetable broth
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 carrots, chopped
    • 2 celery stalks, chopped
    • 1 cup frozen peas
    • 1 cup corn kernels
    • 2 tbsp tomato paste
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tsp thyme
    • 1 tsp rosemary
    • 4 cups mashed potatoes
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    Directions
    1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
    2. In a medium pot, bring the lentils and vegetable broth to a boil.
    3. Reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils are tender. Drain any excess liquid.
    4. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    5. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until softened.
    6. Add the carrots and celery and cook for another 5 minutes.
    7. Stir in the peas, corn, tomato paste, soy sauce, thyme, and rosemary.
    8. Add the cooked lentils and mix well. Season with salt and pepper.
    9. Transfer the lentil mixture to a baking dish and spread the mashed potatoes on top.
    10. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden brown.
    11. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.

    Vegan Energy Balls

    Ingredients
    • 1 cup rolled oats
    • 1/2 cup almond butter
    • 1/4 cup maple syrup
    • 1/4 cup chocolate chips
    • 1/4 cup chia seeds
    • 1/4 cup flax seeds
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • Pinch of salt
    Directions
    1. In a large bowl, mix together all the ingredients until well combined.
    2. Roll the mixture into small balls and place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    3. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or until firm.
    4. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.

    Vegan Chocolate Cake

    Ingredients
    • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
    • 1 tsp baking soda
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1 cup water
    • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
    • 1 tbsp vinegar
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    Directions
    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
    3. Add the water, vegetable oil, vinegar, and vanilla extract and mix until smooth.
    4. Pour the batter into a greased 8-inch round cake pan.
    5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
    6. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
    7. Frost with your favorite vegan frosting and serve.

    Vegan Stuffed Mushrooms

    Ingredients
    • 12 large mushrooms, stems removed
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
    • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
    • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    Directions
    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    3. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until softened.
    4. Stir in the breadcrumbs, nutritional yeast, parsley, salt, and pepper.
    5. Stuff the mushroom caps with the breadcrumb mixture and place on a baking sheet.
    6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the mushrooms are tender and the stuffing is golden brown.

    Vegan Pancakes

    Ingredients
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 2 tbsp sugar
    • 1 tbsp baking powder
    • 1/8 tsp salt
    • 1 cup almond milk
    • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    Directions
    1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
    2. In another bowl, mix the almond milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract.
    3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
    4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease with oil.
    5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
    6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
    7. Serve with maple syrup and fresh fruit.

    Vegan Tacos

    Ingredients
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 bell pepper, chopped
    • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp chili powder
    • 1/2 tsp paprika
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 8 small tortillas
    • 1 avocado, sliced
    • 1/2 cup salsa
    • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
    Directions
    1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    2. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until softened.
    3. Add the bell pepper and cook for another 5 minutes.
    4. Stir in the black beans, cumin, chili powder, paprika, and salt.
    5. Cook for another 5 minutes, or until heated through.
    6. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
    7. Divide the bean mixture among the tortillas.
    8. Top with avocado, salsa, and cilantro.
    9. Serve immediately.

    Vegan Stir-Fry

    Ingredients
    • 1 tbsp sesame oil
    • 1 onion, sliced
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 bell pepper, sliced
    • 1 cup broccoli florets
    • 1 cup snap peas
    • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
    • 1 block firm tofu, cubed
    • 1/4 cup soy sauce
    • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
    • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
    • 1 tsp grated ginger
    • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
    • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
    • Cooked rice, for serving
    Directions
    1. Heat the sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
    2. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until fragrant.
    3. Add the bell pepper, broccoli, snap peas, and mushrooms and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until tender-crisp.
    4. Add the tofu and cook for another 3-4 minutes.
    5. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, and ginger.
    6. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and tofu and stir to coat.
    7. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until heated through.
    8. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.
    9. Serve over cooked rice.
